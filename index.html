TRAVEL

To travel is to untether yourself from the known and drift beautifully into the vast, unwritten narrative of the world. It is the art of trading the comfort of the familiar for the thrill of the foreign, where every sunrise paints a different horizon and the air tastes of spices and salt from distant shores. It is a surrender to the rhythm of wheels on steel or clouds beneath wings, a collection of moments where you feel small under giant stars yet infinitely expanded within. Ultimately, travel is not just about moving across a map, but about letting the map rewrite you, leaving you with a soul stitched together by the threads of a thousand sunsets and the echoes of strangers who became friends.
